Your Earring photo should be here: images/Earring.png  ✅

To add more product photos:
1. Copy your photo into THIS folder
2. Open products.js
3. Find your product and set:
      useImage: true,
      image: "images/YourPhotoName.jpg"

Supported formats: .jpg  .jpeg  .png  .webp

TIPS for best results:
- Square or portrait photos work best (ratio 3:4)
- Recommended size: 800x1066 pixels or similar
- Keep file size under 500KB for fast loading
